E-Recruitment Systems

An e-recruitment system is a web-based tool that automates recruiting processes such as publishing job positions, receiving job applicants' CVs, applicants' pre-screening, ranking and selection among others. Existing e-recruitment systems use keywords or faceted searches to provide better search results to different categories of users (including recruiters, job seekers, hiring agency etc.).

An e-recruitment system as a web-based tool is a merge of algorithms that automates the different requirements of the system. These algorithms take specific inputs from different categories of users to generate results. For instance, a recruiter can provide specific inputs (such as the required experience and education level etc) to the algorithms to rank candidates for an advertised job position.

Generally, e-recruitment systems use several algorithms such as recommender, prediction, semantic matching and filtering algorithms among others. For instance, filtering algorithms use applicants' personality traits to rank applicants for a job position. The inputs for a filtering algorithm are usually specified by recruiters.
