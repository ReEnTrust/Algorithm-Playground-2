Algorithm A

We have three different algorithms that can help job recruiters to shortlist applicants according to their priorities. In the following, we will walk through how our three fictional characters may or may not be shortlisted according to each algorithm.

The presented ranking algorithms rank job applicants based on certain criteria specified by the recruiters. The algorithms assign scores to applicants' attributes and sum up the scores to rank the applicants.

Explanations of Algorithm A Results

This algorithm gives highest priority to the related job experience of the applicants followed by their educational qualification. Here, the recruiter is not concerned about the location of the applicants. Although, Benson has the highest experience score, Elly is given the highest total score because the algorithm considers the cummulative average of both experience and educational qualification scores.
