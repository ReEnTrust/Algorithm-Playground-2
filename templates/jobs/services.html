Selection Algorithms

The implemented algorithms ranks job applicants based on some specified parameters.

The algorithms allow a job employer to specify/change parameters that are relevant for applicants' ranking.

Selection Algorithm_1

This algorithm gives the list of all applicants.

Selection Algorithm_2

This algorithm gives highest priority to applicants' location.

Selection Algorithm_3

This algorithm gives highest priority to educational level.

Selection Algorithm_4

This algorithm gives highest priority to experience.

Selection Algorithm_5

This algorithm allows a job employer to specify/change parameters for selection
