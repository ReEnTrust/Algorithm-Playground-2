Explanations of Algorithm B Results

This algorithm gives highest priority to the applicants' location followed by their related job experience. Here, the recruiter is interested in finding employees that are closer to their current location to avoid relocation cost. Avik is given the highest location score as he resides within 5 miles to the job location unlike Benson that lives over 50 miles away. With this algorithm, Avik is given the highest total score. Elly is given the second highest location score because she lives within 12 miles to the job location. However, Both Benson and Elly have equal total scores because the algorithm considers the cummulative average of their location and experience scores.
